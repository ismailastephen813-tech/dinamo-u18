Chase Shines as Dinamo U18’s Key Player in EMDSL Triumph

May 2026

Chase proved to be one of the most important players for Dinamo U18 throughout the EMDSL campaign, delivering outstanding performances when it mattered most.

Finishing as the league’s top scorer with an impressive 13 goals, Chase consistently led the attack and created problems for every defense he faced. His ability to find space, stay composed in front of goal, and step up in crucial moments made him a standout figure all season.

In the semi-final, Chase once again showed his quality by getting on the scoresheet and helping push the team into the final. But it was in the final where he truly made the difference.

Rising to the occasion, Chase both scored and assisted, playing a direct role in securing the championship for Dinamo U18. His performance in the final highlighted not just his goal-scoring ability, but also his vision and teamwork.

From the first match to the final whistle of the season, Chase was a driving force behind Dinamo U18’s success—cementing his place as one of the most impactful players in the EMDSL this year.
